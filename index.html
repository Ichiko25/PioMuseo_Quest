ABOUT

Welcome to the Museo ni Dr. Pío Valenzuela, a sanctuary of history built upon the very ground where one of our nation's greatest leaders lived.

Born in 1869, Dr. Valenzuela was a physician dedicated to saving lives and a revolutionary leader committed to the birth of a free Philippines.

Dr. Pío Valenzuela (1869–1956)

Physician-patriot and one of the “Big Three” leaders of the Katipunan.
